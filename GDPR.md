# GDPR Compliance
## Data Controller
The data controller for this project is Artem Vashchenko.

Email: ipz231_vai@student.ztu.edu.ua

## Personal Data Collected
This project only stores the nickname you choose for the game. The nickname is saved locally in your browser using localStorage and is used only for displaying the scoreboard.

Additionally, non-personal data is collected, such as:
1. Total playtime
2. Number of games played
3. Number of games won
4. Win rate

## Purpose of Data Processing

The game uses cookies to store your user consent preferences.

The nickname is stored for the purpose of displaying your score and ranking on the games local scoreboard.

Non-personal data is only used to improve the local gameplay experience.

No data is sent to any server or third party.

## Legal Basis

The nickname is stored with your consent, by entering it into the game options.

Cookies are stored based on your consent, which you provide when you first use the game. You can withdraw this consent at any time by deleting or disabling cookies in your browser.

## Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with any third party.

## Data Retention

Data is stored only in your browser and will remain there until you clear your browser storage.

## Age Requirement

This game is intended for users 16 years or older.

Users under 16 should play only under the supervision of a parent or guardian.

## Your Rights

Even with minimal data stored locally, you have the right to:

1. Access your nickname, which is visible in your browser.
2. Delete it by clearing your local storage.
3. Achieve data portability by exporting or copying the data from localStorage.
4. Withdraw consents at any time by clearing or disabling cookies in your browser.

No other rights are applicable since no additional personal data is collected or shared.

## Security Measures

No personal data is transmitted over the network.

The nickname is stored locally in your browser and is not exposed externally.

You are solely responsible for keeping your nicknames private and not sharing them.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be reflected in this document.
